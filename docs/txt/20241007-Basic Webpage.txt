    <h2>Introduction</h2>
    <p>
        PHP, which stands for “PHP: Hypertext Preprocessor,\” is a widely-used open-source scripting language designed primarily for web development12. Created by Rasmus Lerdorf in 1993 and released in 1995, PHP is embedded within HTML and executed on the server, generating dynamic web page content2. It is versatile, supporting various databases and compatible with numerous web servers and operating systems1. PHP’s ease of use, flexibility, and efficiency have made it a popular choice for developing websites and web applications, including major platforms like WordPress1. Its continuous evolution and active community support ensure it remains a robust tool for developers
